You should replace your brake pads every 30,000 to 70,000 miles, depending on your driving habits and vehicle type. Signs that your brake pads need replacement include squeaking or grinding noises, reduced braking performance, or a dashboard brake warning light. For the best safety and performance, have your brake pads inspected regularly, especially if you frequently drive in stop-and-go traffic or tow heavy loads.
